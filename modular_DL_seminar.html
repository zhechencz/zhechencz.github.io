Modular Deligne--Lusztig seminar

Friday 15:00-17:00 during the acadamic year 2017-2018

(Note that there is a graduate level course on modular rep thy of finite groups, lectured by Fei Xu, every Thursday 14:00-16:00 in the first term of the academic year, which should be very helpful for several parts of the seminar.)

Contents

  • Lecture 1 (2017-Sep-1). Intro
  • Lecture 2 (2017-Sep-8). Basic properties of SL_2(F_q) - cardinals, subgroups, conjugacy classes, comparison with SL_2(R), relations with modular forms etc
  • Lecture 3 (2017-Sep-15). Basic properties of the Drinfeld curve and some elementary alg geom revisited - connectedness, smoothness, compactification, gp actions, quotients etc
  • Lecture 4 (2017-Sep-22). Basic properties of the Drinfeld curve II - Frobenius action, point counting, exercises etc
  • Lecture 5 (2017-Oct-12). (plan) Basic rep thy of finite groups revisited - definition, induction, restriction, Frobenius reciprocity, Mackey intertwining formula etc
  • Further contents will appear later
